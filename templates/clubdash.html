About Us

The Rotaract Club of RVCE is a vibrant and dynamic community of young leaders dedicated to creating positive change. As the largest Rotaract club in South East Asia and among the top globally, we proudly represent a diverse group of over 1000 members. Sponsored by Rotary Bangalore South and part of R.I. District 3190, our club is driven by a passion for service, innovation, and leadership. With over 1400 impactful projects to our name, including marquee events like the Paper Drive, Akshara, and the Blood Donation Camp, we are committed to making a meaningful difference in our college, community, and beyond. Our club fosters an environment of collaboration, personal growth, and social responsibility, where every member is empowered to lead with purpose and integrity.

Our Mission

Our mission is to inspire and cultivate a spirit of service, leadership, and fellowship among our members. We are dedicated to empowering young leaders to take initiative, develop their skills, and contribute to society through impactful projects and community service. By fostering a culture of collaboration, innovation, and inclusivity, we strive to make a lasting difference in the lives of those we serve, while also enriching our own lives through personal and professional growth. Together, we are committed to creating a better world, one project at a time.
